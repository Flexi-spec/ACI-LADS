Why We Built This Website

A digital home created by ACI students, for ACI students.

Our Story

As proud students of Ambassadors College Ile-Ife, we saw a gap. While ACI LADS has produced exceptional debaters, writers, and leaders for years, there was no central digital platform where current students could easily access information, register for S-Course, read articles by fellow members, or connect with the executive council.

That changed when the three of us — Ogunbiyi Jesutomisin, Iyiola Eniola, and Olufemi Best Praise — came together with one goal: to build a modern, vibrant, and exclusive online home for ACI LADS.

This website was conceptualized, designed, and built entirely by us during our time at Ambassadors College. Every section, from the S-Course portal to the live Medium articles, was created with one thing in mind: making life easier and more exciting for every current ACI student who wants to develop their voice.

The Minds Behind This Platform

Iyiola Eniola

Olufemi Best Praise

Development & Technical Lead

This Website Was Built For You

If you're a current student of Ambassadors College Ile-Ife and you love debating, writing, or simply want to improve your communication skills — this platform was made for you.

We poured our time, creativity, and passion into this project so that future generations of ACI students can have a strong digital presence for LADS.
