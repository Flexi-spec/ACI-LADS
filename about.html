Shaping Voices That Matter

Since 2009, ACI LADS has been the premier Literary and Debating Society of Ambassadors College. We turn passionate students into confident orators, sharp thinkers, and impactful writers who win competitions and inspire change.

Our Story So Far

2009 — The Spark

A group of visionary students ignited the society with their love for words and debate.

2018 — Rising Stars

First major state and national wins. Membership exploded as word spread.

2024-2025 — Digital Era

Launch of structured S-Course programs and this vibrant online home.

Our Pillars of Excellence

Debating Mastery

Intensive training in logic, rebuttals, and powerful delivery across multiple debate formats.

Literary Power

Creative writing, poetry, essays, and critical thinking that produce publishable work.

Leadership Forge

Building character, teamwork, and ethical leadership for lifelong success.
