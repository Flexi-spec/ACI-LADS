Our Legacy

ACI LADS is the Literary and Debating Society of Ambassadors College Ile-Ife—a forefront of spoken and written excellence. Founded to instill eloquence, critical thinking, and creative expression, our society upholds the proud tradition of student leadership, academic distinction, and artistic accomplishment.

Motto

"Excellence in Speech, Brilliance in Writing."

Our Philosophy

At the heart of ACI LADS lies an unwavering pursuit of literary royalty—where articulate thought and imaginative penmanship meet character and service. We believe every voice deserves a stage and every story can ignite change. Through friendly contests, symposia, and drama, we foster a culture of courage, wit, and friendship, empowering Ambassadors who debate not only to win, but to inspire.
