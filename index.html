Our Story

Shaping Voices,

Founded within the hallowed halls of Ambassadors College, ACI LADS has stood as the premier platform for literary excellence and oratory mastery. We transform students into eloquent speakers, critical thinkers, and brilliant writers.

Through debate tournaments, essay competitions, and literary workshops, our society has consistently produced voices that resonate far beyond the classroom.
